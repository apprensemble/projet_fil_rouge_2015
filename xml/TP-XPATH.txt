TP xpath

Le fichier bookmark-raw-dup.xml contient des doublons par rapport au
fichier bookmark-raw.xml pour les besoins de ce TP.

xpath -e "//url" bookmark-raw.xml 
xpath -e "//url[@keyname=\"google\"" bookmark-raw.xml 
xpath -e "//langage/*/url" bookmark-raw.xml 
xpath -e "//langage" bookmark-raw.xml 
xpath -e "//langage/*/url" bookmark-raw.xml 
xpath -e "//langage/*/url[@keyname=\"ncsa\"]" bookmark-raw.xml 

xpath -e "//url" bookmark-raw-dup.xml 
xpath -e "//langage/*/url" bookmark-raw-dup.xml
xpath -e "//java/url" bookmark-raw-dup.xml
xpath -e "//url[@keyname=\"sun\"]" bookmark-raw-dup.xml
